🌱 Post-attention economy

Jul 02, 2026 | 2 min read

These companies are moving us from the Attention Economy to the Attachment Economy. This new economy isn’t interested in collecting our clicks or browsing habits. It’s focused on gathering our inner world: our fears, our desires, our confessions, and our emotional patterns. https://josebriones.substack.com/p/the-coming-waves-of-digital-addiction?utm_source=%2Finbox%2Fsaved&utm_medium=reader2

Objective

Map what succeeds attention as the primary extraction mechanism when overstimulation makes attention both scarce and resistant. Examine the two leading successor models: attachment economy (manufactured connection) and intention economy (behavioural influence).

Why this topic

Attention economy is hitting a ceiling. Understanding what comes next is both theoretically interesting and practically relevant for anticipating how digital products, marketing, and platforms will be designed in the next cycle.

Working thesis

The shift into post-attention economies is a slippery slope, accelerated by AI. As friction is progressively eliminated, individuals increasingly outsource thinking, effort, and decision-making to AI systems. This erodes personal agency over time, and that erosion is precisely what allows attachment and intention economies to thrive. The threat is not a single moment of adoption but a gradual, barely perceptible loss of capacity to resist.

Current stage

Early. Previous notes on intention economy, no active reading or formed position.

Key tensions

Attachment economy may be more emotionally invasive than attention extraction. Intention economy requires more granular behavioural data to function. Both represent an escalation, not a retreat, from current extraction models. AI accelerates both.

Open questions

  • How can customers directly signal intent to buy in an intention economy without that signal being co-opted?
  • What do companies vs consumers actually gain from each economy type, and is it ever balanced?
  • How can consumers become more aware of what is happening and what options they have?
