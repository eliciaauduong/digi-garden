🌿 Project 0

Jul 02, 2026 | 2 min read

What is project 0?

A collaborative community to explore what you enjoy, build a portfolio and reshape work around your interests and values. Where you’re celebrated for trying, and collaborators help bring your ideas to life.

The problem

  • Eggs in one basket: You’re dependent on a single company for your livelihood. With AI and redundancies on the rise, that’s a risky move.
  • No compass: It’s hard to land your dream job when you don’t know what that is or what that could look like beyond the traditional 9-5.
  • The bubble trap: You’re surviving in your current job but it doesn’t feel like your calling. You’re too burned out to figure out what’s next.
  • Grass is greener: You have an idea of what you could enjoy, but you haven’t tried it and completely switching jobs feel too risky without proof.
  • Catch 22: You have no experience in the field you want but to have any experience you need an opportunity to gain experience.

Who it's for

People who are looking to:

  • explore and test different ideas to learn more about what they value or enjoy vs don't
  • try something new and build proof that it is actually aligned to you before you completely change career direction
  • have a space to figure out what they want with their career
  • build skills and visiblity outside your job so that you're not dependent on one company for your livelihood
  • build a portfolio of work that demonstrates what you can do, without waiting for someone to give you a chance

Coming soon

  • Talks on the ground: share your expertise, everybody has something to teach/share
  • Projects: dedicated time to work on your projects and find collaborators to explore something you're genuinely interested in
  • Community sessions: quarterly reviews, co-working sessions, and hangouts to stay connected and accountable.
  • Workshops: learn skills and frameworks you can add to your portfolio or use in your work, built by and for people looking for more
