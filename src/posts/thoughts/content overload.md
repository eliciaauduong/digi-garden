## Curation premium
objective: how do we decide what is worth learning, making, and choosing in an era where the cost of generation is zero but the cost of attention is at an all-time high?
- **Essay:**["Curating as Resistance in the Age of AI"](https://www.on-curating.org/issues/issue-64/issue-64-reader/curating-as-resistance-in-the-age-of-ai.html) via On-Curating. It argues that the curator's role is no longer to "provide meaning" but to "design generative conditions."
- **Newsletter:****Dirt** (dirt.substack.com). A great example of "cultural curation" that successfully navigates the noise of the digital attention economy.
- **Mental Model:** The **"Lindy Effect."** Study this to understand which information has "durable trust" and "compounding value" vs. what is just "ephemeral noise."
